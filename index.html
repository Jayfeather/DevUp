Advance Projects and Reach of Social Change

We want to address two lingering problems:
Problem 1) Who needs web and mobile application development the most?
the people who can't afford it!
Problem 2) Who can create web and mobile applications?
skilled software engineers who are hard to find and in demand!

DevUp will build actionable web/mobile software platforms on behalf of social good organizations that maximize community impact. We will drive projects through a mentorship program that enhances junior developer skills, thus preparing them for future professional opportunities.
